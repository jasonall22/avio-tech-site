Our Services

TV Wall Mounting & Home Theater Installation

We specialize in secure TV wall mount installation, including over-the-fireplace TV mounting, concealed wire TV mounting, and soundbar mounting. We also design and install complete home theater systems with surround sound, audio system calibration, media room design, and projector and screen installation, giving you a clean, professional setup that looks and sounds incredible.

Smart Home, Cameras & Wi-Fi

Upgrade your home with smart lighting, smart thermostat setup, security camera installation, and video doorbell installation. We handle smart home device install, Wi-Fi network upgrades, and network optimization so your streaming devices, cameras, and automations run smoothly in every room of your home.

Computer Setup & Tech Support

We provide computer setup and support, software installation, printer setup, and Wi-Fi troubleshooting to keep your home office running smoothly. From software updates and device configuration to resolving slow internet or dropped connections, we make sure your technology works the way it should.
